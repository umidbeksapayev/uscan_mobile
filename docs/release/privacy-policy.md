ma'lumotlarni yig'ishimiz va ulardan qanday foydalanishimizni tushuntiradi.

### Yig'iladigan ma'lumotlar
- **Email manzili** — hisobga kirish (autentifikatsiya) uchun.
- **Do'kon ma'lumotlari** — mahsulot katalogi, sotuv tarixi, mijozlar (nasiya), kirim/
  ta'minotchi yozuvlari. Bularni do'kon egasi/kassir o'zi kiritadi.

### Qurilma ruxsatlari
- **Kamera** — faqat mahsulot shtrix-kodini skanerlash va mahsulot rasmini olish uchun

## English

**uscan** ("we") is a point-of-sale (POS) application for small shop owners. This policy
explains what data we collect and how we use it.

### Data we collect
- **Email address** — for sign-in (authentication).
- **Shop data** — product catalog, sales history, customers (credit), supply records.
  This is entered by the shop owner/cashier.

### Device permissions
- **Camera** — only to scan product barcodes and capture product photos (you grant
  permission each time).
- **Photos/Gallery** — only to upload a product image you select.
- **Bluetooth** — only to connect to a receipt printer you choose (Android).

### Data we DO NOT collect
We never collect location, contacts, SMS, call logs, **microphone/audio**, or browsing
history. The app contains **no advertising or analytics SDKs**.

### Where data is stored
Data is stored on **Supabase** (backend service). Each shop's data is isolated via Row
Level Security (RLS). All traffic is encrypted over HTTPS.

### Third-party services
- **Supabase** — authentication, database, and file storage.
- No other third parties (ads, analytics, trackers) are used.
